قوة عقلك الباطن

Author: الدكتور جوزيف ميرفي

"The Power of Your Subconscious Mind" by Dr. Joseph Murphy explores the incredible potential of the subconscious mind to heal, solve problems, and achieve goals. The book explains how the subconscious mind works and provides practical techniques and exercises to harness its power. Murphy emphasizes the importance of positive thinking, visualization, and affirmations to reprogram the subconscious and bring about positive changes in all areas of life, including health, wealth, and relationships. It's a guide to understanding and utilizing the hidden forces within to create a more fulfilling and successful life.
