Introduction to Fluid Mechanics

Author: John W. Mitchel

"Fox and McDonald's Introduction to Fluid Mechanics" by John W. Mitchell is a widely used textbook that provides a comprehensive and accessible introduction to the fundamental principles of fluid mechanics. This tenth edition covers essential topics such as fluid properties, fluid statics, kinematics, control volume analysis, differential analysis of fluid flow, dimensional analysis, and flow in pipes and ducts. It is known for its clear writing style, numerous worked examples, and practical applications that help students develop a strong understanding of the subject matter.
