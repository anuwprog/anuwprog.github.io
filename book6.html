Elementary Linear Algebra

Author: Ron Larson

"Elementary Linear Algebra" by Ron Larson is a textbook designed for introductory courses in linear algebra. This edition, marked as "8e," provides a comprehensive overview of the fundamental concepts of linear algebra, including systems of linear equations, matrices, determinants, vector spaces, eigenvalues, and eigenvectors.
