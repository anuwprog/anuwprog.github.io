Rich Dad Poor Dad.

Author: Robert T. Kiyosak

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" is a personal finance book that contrasts the financial philosophies of Robert Kiyosaki's two "dads": his biological father (the "poor dad") and his wealthy friend's father (the "rich dad"). The book challenges conventional wisdom about money, work, and investing. Kiyosaki advocates for financial literacy, building assets that generate passive income, and understanding how money truly works to achieve financial independence.
