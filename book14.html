David Copperfield

Author: Charles Dickens

"David Copperfield" is a classic novel by Charles Dickens that follows the life and experiences of its titular character, David Copperfield, from his childhood to adulthood. Narrated in the first person, the book chronicles David's journey through various hardships and triumphs, including his difficult early life, his time in a boarding school, his career aspirations, and his romantic relationships. It is a semi-autobiographical work, reflecting elements of Dickens' own life, and explores themes of social class, family, love, and the challenges of growing up in Victorian England.
