Great Expectations

Author: Charles Dickens

"Great Expectations" is a classic coming-of-age novel that follows the life of Pip, an orphan who unexpectedly comes into a large fortune and moves to London to become a gentleman. The story explores themes of social class, ambition, love, and moral development. Pip's "great expectations" lead him on a journey of self-discovery, where he learns about the true nature of wealth and happiness, and the importance of loyalty and compassion.
