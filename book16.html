The Pickwick Papers

Author: Charles Dickens

"The Pickwick Papers" is Charles Dickens' first novel, a humorous and episodic account of the travels and adventures of Mr. Samuel Pickwick and the members of his Pickwick Club. The book follows their various comical encounters and misadventures as they journey through the English countryside. Initially published in serial form, it is known for its lively characters, satirical observations of English society, and its blend of farce and sentimentality.
