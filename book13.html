Great Expectations

Author: Charles Dickens

"Great Expectations" is a classic novel by Charles Dickens that follows the life of Pip, an orphan who unexpectedly comes into a large fortune and moves to London to become a gentleman. The story explores themes of social class, ambition, love, and moral development as Pip navigates his new life and discovers the true source of his wealth and the expectations that come with it.
